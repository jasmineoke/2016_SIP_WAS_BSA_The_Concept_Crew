WAYS TO HEAL EMOTIONALLY

Many people ask what they can do to get over a sexual assault and many experts recommend seeking medical care. However, studies have shown that it is very important to see a doctor first either to collect evidence or to just make sure you are okay and feel safer in your body.


After this, you may decide to seek therapy to heal emotionally. You have just gone through a terrible incident, you feel as though you are at fault , and you feel invaded and it is okay. You ought to be . At this point, you may want to press charges against your attacker and that is acceptable however do not do it until you feel ready to. For more information on legal assistance click here. You can also reach out to friends and family members you trust for support especially since you might be feeling isolated and alone. You are not alone. Do not be afraid and there are a lot of people who unfortunately have gone through horrible incidents like this. You can get through this. I believe in you.



If you do want to go to therapy, you can always attend individual sessions or in group. This way you can share your feelings and learn how to use your feelings in other way and possibly even make friends with people who understand you and can make you happier.













IF YOU SEEK LEGAL ASSISTANCE

So you are here because you have decided to seek justice for yourself and be a voice for other survivors.Now that you are ready to press criminal charges against the assaulter,it is important you know a few things: You can ask your landlord to change the locks on your home if you do not feel secure. If you need to leave work because of your safety, you may still be eligible to get unemployment compensation benefits. If you are undocumented but have been a victim of any sex crime, you and your children may be eligible for a special visa to stay in the U.S. For more information, do call the National Sexual Assault Hotline at 1-800-656-HOPE













WAYS TO HEAL PHYSICALLY

If you feel like your self-confidence has reduced because of your experience, it is important to know that it is normal to feel that way. An intruder just invaded your body, of course you feel damaged. But your spirit is not damaged and you can keep fighting.However, if you want to boost your self-confidence, try the following tips:



Exercising has proved to be a stress reducer and it makes you feel better and happier. So next time you feel the blues, do a few pullups or something like that.


Meditation has also proven to make you feel calmer and happier. Just stay quiet for two minutes and empty your mind. Also turn off all distractions . It makes you feel better and helps concentration.


Drink Water. You should already be drinking half of your body weight amount of water. Water is great stuff. It actually makes your skin clearer and clears your stomach of bad stuff. I mean, it sounds amazing. You should try it. If you already try it, keep doing it.


Smile. We understand this is a trying time for you but remeber if you are still able to read this post, it means you are alive so just smile. Think about it, you smiling can make someone else who is sad smile, meaning you have just made someone else happy. How cool is that! So whatever situation you find yourself, just smile * insert Shia Labeouf video *
